Doki Doggie


Oh no! the black guy have kidnapped the little boy, rescuing him by beat the big boss with the only ball!

 

Mouse left clik to throw ball when you get one.

'A'. 'D' to move left and right, press twice to dash.

"R" to restart the level.


Made in 48hr game competition, Ludum Dare.

The theme is "You Only Get One".
